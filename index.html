About Campaign

The Hebron High School Band is a state and national award-winning organization since 1999 with over 350 students. It is the largest school organization in our Hebron community and represents families from Carrollton, Lewisville, Frisco, and Plano. Being a part of the Hebron Band program provides students with valuable experiences they will carry with them their entire lives.

Teamwork Self-Discipline Leadership Communal Goal Setting

These life skills benefit our students long after they have left the marching field and prepare them for future success in college, career, and business.

Costs of keeping the band program running are significant and our financial need is greater than ever. We count on donations to keep our program growing and thriving.

The Hebron Band Booster Club is a registered 501(c) (3) nonprofit, volunteer organization committed to enriching our local children's high school band experience.
